Our Mission

Have you ever been overwhelmed by all the different choices in flower shops? Stress no more. Kaylee’s flowers offer the highest quality flowers and succulents in beautiful custom arrangements. Kaylee Pierson originally founded Kaylee’s in 2015 after realizing most people didn’t know what kind of flowers they wanted for certain events. Kaylee’s makes the process super easy and straightforward by providing you a couple of different options to choose from based on the information you give about your preferences
