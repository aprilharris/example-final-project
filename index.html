Flowers for any occasion

Kaylee's Flowers specializes in unique and beautiful floral arrangements. At Kaylee's, we provide customers around with an online marketplace of beautiful bouquets, long-lasting plants, and gourmet gift baskets.

Who We Work With

CalFlowers

Kaylee's Flowers is an active member of CalFlowers. Lately, we've used our membership to promote consumer access to fresh and safe flower products.


California Poppy Festival

We're a proud sponsor of the California Poppy Festival. We invite you to stop by our table this year.


University of California Health

Every year, our team transforms UC Health hospital lobbies with thousands of stunning flowers & lush plants which visitors can purchase onsite. KF donates $1 of every purchase to UC Davis Children’s Hospital.


California Afterchool Network

Thanks to the California Afterschool Network, we're able to host weekly floriculture workshops during the spring semester at local public schools. Students learn about California's floral industry in addition to participating in creative floral design activities.
